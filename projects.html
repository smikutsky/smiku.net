    AACC Mechatronics Capstone Project

    My mechatronics capstone project at AACC was to get an ABB IRB 120 industrial robot to draw using multiple colors of dry erase markers. My partner and I designed and developed the marker changer attachment for the robot, as well as programmed the robot to draw.
