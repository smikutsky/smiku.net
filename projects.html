    • Drawing Robot

    AACC Mechatronics Capstone Project, January-May 2022

    My mechatronics capstone project at AACC was to get an ABB IRB 120 industrial robot to draw using multiple colors of dry erase markers. My partner and I designed, 3D printed, and developed the marker changer attachment for the robot, as well as programmed the robot to draw. We programmed the robot to draw the AACC riverhawk logo, Ukraine, as well as a line art drawing.


    Light Cube

    Personal Project, February 2020
    Error playing video

    My 5×5×5 LED light cube was a project inspired by another light cube I had seen. The project is powered by an Arduino Nano connected to shift registers to handle the additional outputs. I first built a smaller and rougher 4×4×4 cube that's effects were programmed by moifying the byte sent to the shift registers mathematically. The larger and cleaner 5×5×5 cube included drawing functions to make programming of effects much easier.
